Our Story

At the heart of Hill Top – Kumasi, we’ve built a space where style meets skill and every client feels seen.

Our journey began not in boardrooms, but in the vibrant energy of our training years — long hours of learning, creating, and perfecting our craft through hands-on apprenticeships.


Those experiences taught us that beauty is personal, and that the right touch can change more than just appearance — it can change confidence. But we also saw that our community was missing something: a modern salon that could deliver both trend-forward looks and an unforgettable experience.


So we created Hagan Salon— a place where artistry meets warmth. From precision braiding to luxurious nail care and head-turning special occasion styles, we bring global inspiration home to Kumasi, crafting looks as unique as the people who wear them.
